Frequently Asked Questions

Our minimum rental period is 1 day (24 hours). We also offer weekly and monthly packages with special discounted rates for longer rentals.
All our rentals include comprehensive insurance, 24/7 roadside assistance, basic maintenance, and a full tank of fuel (return full). Additional services like GPS, child seats, and drivers are available at extra cost.
